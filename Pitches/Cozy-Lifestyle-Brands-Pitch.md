## Campaign Brief — "My Cozy Edit" (Send to Any Brand That Says Yes)

### Concept: "Things That Make My Home Feel Good"

**Format:** 2-piece content series
1. **Reel/TikTok:** "My cozy edit — everything that makes my home feel like a sanctuary." Pan across: Cozy Earth pajamas, Warmies on the couch, Barefoot Dreams blanket, ambient lighting, warm drink. Lifestyle video, no hard sell.
2. **Storefront Section:** "My Cozy Edit" — dedicated section, all products linked, updated seasonally.

**Why this works:**
- High saves — aspirational home comfort content drives repeat views
- Gifting traffic — "what should I get her?" searches convert on this type of content
- Storefront completeness — adds a lifestyle dimension beyond fitness/wellness

**Deliverables:** 1 TikTok/Reel + Story mention + Amazon storefront listing
**Timeline:** 2 weeks from product receipt (fastest turnaround deal type)
**Affiliate:** Amazon storefront (3-8% depending on category) + brand affiliate if available

The value here is storefront completeness and lifestyle positioning, not the per-post revenue. These deals round out Nicki's "My Cozy Edit" section and create a gift-season revenue stream that compounds every November-December.
