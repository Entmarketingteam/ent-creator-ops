## Campaign Brief (Send Once Connected)

### Concept: "The Part of My Recovery Routine Nobody Talks About"

**The insight:** Everyone talks about supplements, massage guns, cold plunge. Nobody talks about what they put on their feet after a workout — and the feet/arch impact has a disproportionate effect on full-body recovery.

**Content pieces:**
1. **Hero Reel:** "My full recovery routine" — recovery slides as the first step literally. Show them going on right after the gym, the arch support, the "my feet thank me" angle.
2. **Educational piece:** "Why what you wear after a workout matters" — brief science on impact absorption, plantar fascia, arch support. Makes the purchase feel smart, not indulgent.
3. **Lifestyle piece:** "What I wear around the house" — normalizes recovery slides as everyday footwear, not just a gym thing. Wide lifestyle appeal.

**Deliverables:** 2-3 Reels/TikToks + Story mentions
**Timeline:** 4 weeks from product receipt
**Affiliate:** Amazon storefront + brand affiliate program if available
