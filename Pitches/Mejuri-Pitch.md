## Campaign Brief (Send Once Connected)

### Concept: "My Everyday Stack"

**The insight:** Jewelry purchase decisions for this audience aren't driven by "it's beautiful." They're driven by "does it go with everything, does it last, and is it worth it?" Creator content that answers those three questions converts better than any editorial shoot.

**Content pieces:**

1. **"What I Wear Every Day" Reel:** Nicki shows her daily jewelry routine — what goes on first, what layers, what she never takes off. Mejuri pieces front and center. No try-on haul energy — just genuine "this is my stack" authenticity.

2. **"Is Mejuri Worth It?" Honest Review:** Address the elephant in the room — it's not $30 jewelry, but it's also not $3,000 jewelry. What do you actually get? Durability, tarnish test (if applicable), how it holds up. This is the piece that drives conversions from people who are on the fence.

3. **"How I Style Fine Jewelry" Quick Tip:** Stacking guide — how to wear multiple pieces without looking overdone. Lifestyle format, high saves. Ends with storefront link.

**Deliverables:** 2-3 TikToks/Reels + Story mentions
**Timeline:** 3 weeks from product receipt
**Affiliate:** Mejuri has an affiliate program — push for 15%+ given the price point ($80-300 per piece)
