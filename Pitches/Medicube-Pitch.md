## Campaign Brief

### Campaign Concept: "Why Everyone in Korea Uses This" / K-Beauty Education Series

**Angle:** Nicki discovers Medicube and documents what she learns — educational content that builds brand credibility while driving purchase.

**Content Arc (4-week series):**

**Week 1 — "What is Medicube?" (Awareness)**
- Hero Reel: "The Korean skincare brand everyone in the US is just finding out about"
- Intro to the brand, unboxing haul, first impressions
- Focuses on: Zero Cleansing Foam + Zero Pore Pad (accessible entry products)

**Week 2 — "The Device" (Consideration)**
- Hero Reel: AGE-R device demo — extremely visual, "does it actually work?" format
- Before/after skin texture
- This is the highest content-to-conversion piece — device demos are proven viral format

**Week 3 — "The Ingredient I'd Never Heard Of" (Education)**
- Reel: PDRN explained for a US audience — "salmon DNA in my skincare sounds insane, here's why it works"
- Rides the PDRN search wave (165K+ monthly searches, +77% growth)
- Amazon storefront integration: PDRN Ampoule + PDRN Mask

**Week 4 — "My Full Medicube Routine" (Conversion)**
- "30 days with Medicube — what I kept, what I skipped, and what actually changed my skin"
- Brings it all together, strongest affiliate/purchase moment

**Deliverables per week:** 1 Reel/TikTok + Story set
**Total:** 4 Reels, 4 Story sets, ongoing storefront integration
