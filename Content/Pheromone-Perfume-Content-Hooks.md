# Pheromone Perfume — Content Strategy for Nicki
**ENT Agency | March 2026**
**Platform:** TikTok primary, Instagram Reels secondary
**Objective:** Affiliate revenue (Amazon storefront) + brand deal proof-of-concept

---

## Why This Is the Play

- **415,000 monthly Amazon searches** — enormous demand with almost no quality creator content
- TikTok-to-Amazon purchase funnel is the most proven in the platform's history for this product type
- No dominant creator in the space yet — this is a first-mover moment
- Average product price: $15-35 → high volume purchases = strong affiliate run rate
- "Does it actually work?" curiosity format is one of the highest-performing TikTok structures

## Content Series: "I Tested Pheromone Perfume for 30 Days"

### Piece 1 — The Hook (Post First)
**Format:** TikTok Reel, 30-45 seconds
**Structure:** Curiosity → Setup → Cliffhanger

**Script framework:**
> "Okay I need to talk about pheromone perfume because I've been wearing it for three weeks and something is happening that I can't fully explain."
>
> [show product]
>
> "Pheromone perfume is designed to trigger attraction — it contains synthetic pheromones, which are the chemical signals your body naturally produces. The idea is you're basically amplifying your biological signal."
>
> "I was fully skeptical. I ordered three different ones from Amazon. Here's what I noticed..."
>
> [cut] "I'll link the ones I'm testing in my storefront — but watch this series before you buy because not all of them are the same."

**Caption:** "I've been testing pheromone perfume for 3 weeks and I need to tell you what's happening 👀 links in bio/storefront #pheromone #pheromones #pheromonetest #perfume #fragrancetok"

---

### Piece 2 — The Comparison (Post Week 1-2)
**Format:** TikTok Reel, 45-60 seconds
**Structure:** Product review — compare 3 options across key dimensions

**Script framework:**
> "I bought three pheromone perfumes at different price points and tested them all. Here's my breakdown."
>
> [Product 1 — budget under $20]:
> "This one: very light, more of a skin scent, subtle. The pheromone concentration is lower. Good starter if you're skeptical."
>
> [Product 2 — mid $20-35]:
> "This is my current favorite. [describe scent]. The staying power is actually impressive. This is the one I've been wearing daily."
>
> [Product 3 — premium]:
> "Strongest scent, most noticeable. If you want something you'll actually smell all day, this is it."
>
> "All three are linked in my Amazon storefront under pheromone perfume. I'll tell you which one had the most interesting results in my next video."

**Caption:** "Tested 3 pheromone perfumes so you don't have to — here's the honest breakdown 🧪 all linked in storefront #pheromone #perfumereview #amazonfind"

---

### Piece 3 — "The Results" (Post Week 3-4)
**Format:** TikTok Reel, 60-90 seconds — this is the conversion piece
**Structure:** Results + honest take + recommendation

**Script framework:**
> "30 days of pheromone perfume. Here's my actual honest take."
>
> "Does it work the way the TikToks say? Here's what I actually noticed..."
>
> [Give honest, specific observations — more compliments from husband/partner? More confident? Reactions in public? Keep it genuine and relatable, not exaggerated]
>
> "Here's what I think is actually happening — [explain the psychology: scent is the most direct route to the limbic system, confidence from wearing something you believe in has real behavioral effects, pheromone concentration matters]"
>
> "My actual recommendation: [name the winner]. I've been wearing it every day for two weeks. [Specific detail about why]. It's [price] on Amazon and it's genuinely in my daily rotation now."
>
> "Everything I tested is in my storefront — link in bio."

**Caption:** "30 days of pheromone perfume — my honest results 🧴 not what I expected. Linked in storefront. #pheromone #pheromonetest #perfume #amazonfinds #fragrancereview"

---

### Piece 4 — The "Stack" (Bonus, Post Separately)
**Format:** 30 seconds max, impulse purchase format
**Structure:** "My current fragrance stack"

> "My current scent rotation: [morning fragrance] for daytime, [pheromone pick] whenever I actually want to be noticed. Sounds unhinged, works every time."

**Why this piece:** Short, punchy, high repost potential. Different audience entry point — less "30-day experiment," more "this is just what I do."
