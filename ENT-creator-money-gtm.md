## SCENARIO 1: THE LMNT MACHINE
### $560,000+ From One Brand. 47 Deals. 4 Years Running.

The surface story: Nicki posts about an electrolyte drink.
The ENT story: A single intro turned into a $560K revenue stream still active today.

| Year | Deals | Revenue |
|------|-------|---------|
| 2022 | 8 deals | $75,124 |
| 2024 | ~17 deals | $203,370 |
| 2025 | ~18 deals | $230,010 |
| 2026 | ~4 deals YTD | $52,000 |
| TOTAL | 47 deals | $560,504 |

What ENT did differently:
- Negotiated usage rights on every deal (adds 20-50% to base rate)
- Secured TikTok Spark Ad rights where LMNT paid to boost Nicki content as ads
- Got paid for allowlisting: brand runs ads FROM Nicki account
- Stacked story share deals on top of primary paid posts
- Renegotiated rates upward each contract cycle

Avg deal value grew from ~$9,300 in 2022 to ~$13,000 in 2025.

## SCENARIO 6: THE USAGE RIGHTS MULTIPLIER
### How One Contract Clause Is Worth $20K+

Most creators give usage rights away for free. ENT standard practice:
- 30-day usage rights: +25% of base rate
- 90-day usage rights: +50% of base rate
- TikTok Spark Ads: separate flat fee $500-$2,000 per post
- Allowlisting: 25-40% of base rate per month

Real example, Hiya 2024: A $2,500 base deal with 90-day usage rights + TikTok Spark Ad = $5,250 total. Without ENT negotiation: $2,500. With ENT: $5,250. 110% more money from the same post.
