## The Ingredients — What You Need to Know

### PDRN (Polydeoxyribonucleotide)
**What it is:** Purified DNA fragments derived from salmon sperm/eggs. Used medically in South Korea for wound healing and skin regeneration for 20+ years. Crossing to cosmetic use in the US now.

**Why it works:** Stimulates fibroblasts (the cells that produce collagen and elastin). Accelerates skin repair, reduces inflammation, improves texture and tone.

**Consumer-friendly explanation:** "It's basically a skin healing signal — telling your cells to repair and rebuild. Korean dermatologists have been using it in medical settings for decades. It's just now available in skincare."

**Products to feature:**
- PDRN ampoule / serum (most clinical format, highest purchase intent)
- PDRN mask (accessible entry point, easy content)
- PDRN cream (moisturizer format — easiest to explain)

**Who it's for:** Anyone focused on: anti-aging, skin texture, dullness, post-breakout healing, redness reduction. Wide appeal.

**Content caution:** Don't overclaim. Stick to "stimulates skin repair" and "Korean clinical ingredient" framing. Don't say "reverses aging."

---

### GHK-CU (Copper Peptide)
**What it is:** A naturally occurring copper complex. One of the most studied peptides in skincare — decades of research behind it. Promotes collagen synthesis, antioxidant, anti-inflammatory.

**Why it's trending now:** GHK-CU searches are at 550K/mo. It's being positioned as the "upgrade" from retinol for people who want results without irritation.

**Consumer-friendly explanation:** "A peptide your body already produces — but levels decline with age. Applying it topically signals your skin to produce more collagen and repair itself. Think of it like giving your skin the instruction manual it forgot."

**Products to feature:**
- Copper peptide serums (multiple brands on Amazon)
- GHK-CU as an ingredient in multi-peptide serums

**Who it's for:** Mature skin, anti-aging, texture, firmness. Also sensitive skin who can't tolerate retinol.

## FAQ — What If Someone Asks...

**"Is it safe?"**
PDRN has 20+ years of clinical use in South Korea. GHK-CU is naturally occurring in the body. Both are well-studied. Patch test as with any new skincare ingredient.

**"Where do I buy it?"**
Amazon storefront (easiest), also Soko Glam, YesStyle, brand websites. Always direct to your affiliate link first.

**"Is this better than [retinol / hyaluronic acid / niacinamide]?"**
Different mechanism, different goals. PDRN/GHK-CU are repair and collagen-stimulating — they complement actives, they don't replace everything.

**"How long does it take to work?"**
Most clinical studies show skin improvement at 4-8 weeks with consistent use.
